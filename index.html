DIY Succulent Terrarium Kit

Comes with a glass bowl, soil, pebbles, moss, and two succulents to build your own mini terrarium. This kit is perfect for beginners who want a fun, hands-on way to learn about plant care. The included step-by-step guide makes it easy to assemble, and the finished terrarium doubles as a unique piece of home décor. With minimal watering required, it’s a low-maintenance way to bring a touch of greenery into your space. Included is a care guide to help you nurture your new plants, a set of assorted Succulent cuttings, and all the materials you need to create your own beautiful terrarium.
